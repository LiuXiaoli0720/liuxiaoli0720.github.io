Submission in Progress CCF-A
SpikeMoE: Towards Cross-Scale Brain-Inspired Computing with Flexible Mixture-of-Experts
Xiaoli Liu, Yujie Liang, Jialin Li, Chenxiao Dou, Malu Zhang, Yang Yang
This work proposes SpikeMoE, an energy-efficient, cross-scale brain-inspired computing framework that integrates Spiking Neural Networks and Mixture-of-Experts via a novel competition-inhibition mechanism and a missing-modality modeling strategy to achieve robust, high performance in multi-sensory scenarios.

Submission in Progress CCF-A
Contrastive Attention Mitigates Spectral Bias in Spiking Transformers
Xiaoli Liu, Jialin Li, Yujie Liang, Jieyuan Zhang, Yimeng Shan, Kexin Shi, Chenxiao Dou, Malu Zhang, Yang Yang
This work identifies the high-frequency information loss in Spiking Transformers caused by their inherent low-pass filtering nature and proposes a biologically-inspired Spiking Contrastive Attention (SCA) paradigm to efficiently recover these components, consistently boosting performance across diverse visual tasks.
📄 Paper ｜ Code
Submission in Progress CCF-A
Temporal Predictive Coding for Training-Free ANN-to-SNN Conversion
Xiaoli Liu, Yujie Liang, Jialin Li, Chenxiao Dou, Malu Zhang, Yang Yang
This work proposes a brain-inspired Temporal Predictive Coding (TPC) framework for highly efficient ANN-to-SNN conversion that leverages temporal dynamics for state prediction and error correction, achieving state-of-the-art performance with significantly reduced latency and energy consumption across vision, language, and multimodal tasks.
